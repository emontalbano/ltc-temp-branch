Fingerprint Sign On is almost ready

Sign into your account using your fingerprint

You can choose to sign into the John Hancock app using your fingerprint. Want to check in with a client but you don't have an internet connection? Fingerprint sign on allows you to log into your account and make certain changes. Once you reconnect, your changes will sync automatically.

This feature can be enabled or disabled in your app Settings at any time.
